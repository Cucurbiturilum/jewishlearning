teachers

rabbi yankie denburg

Rabbi Yankie Denburg grew up in Coral Springs and is a graduate of the Rabbinical College of America. He has studied in Israel and has spent several years after his studies assisting in the communities of South Africa, Zimbabwe, India, and China.

Married with seven kids, he is the founder and Spiritual Director of the Chabad Jewish Center in Coral Springs. Rabbi Denburg's greatest passion is teaching. and sharing the depth, beauty and life lessons we all can internalize from the timeless wisdom of the Torah and it's more mystical, spiritual dimensions.

dr. sandra lilienthal

Dr. Sandra Lilienthal was born and raised in Brazil, where she received her MBA in Marketing and Strategic Planning. After almost 10 years in the business world, Sandra went through a major change in careers, and for the last 22 years has been working with Jewish Education in many different capacities: from Religious School teacher and Bar/Bat Mitzvah Tutor, to Director of Education of two synagogues, to Director of Lifelong Learning.

She has taught every age from preschool to adults, both in Brazil and in the USA (Waco, TX and South Florida). In the past 8 years, Sandra has focused on teaching adults in South Florida and completed her doctoral dissertation on Adult Jewish Education.

Sandra holds a Masters in Jewish Studies and a Doctorate in Jewish Education. She the author of the Adult Education two-year curriculum used by the Broward Central Agency for Jewish Education, designed with the 21st century learner in mind. Sandra is also part of the Florence Melton Adult Mini-School faculty, and co-leads the Family Chanukah with her husband, Rabbi Alejandro Lilienthal. Sandra has great enthusiasm for teaching Judaism as a living religion, and infusing true passion for it amongst her students.

chana denburg

Chana Denburg is the mother of 7 beautiful children. Originally from Brooklyn, Chana received her Masters Degree in Special Education and is one of those truly loving and caring individuals who creates an instant connection with anyone she meets.

She is the Director of the well-acclaimed Chabad Hebrew School and the Program Director at Chabad, who not only plans all the Holiday and Special Events but also the extremely popular Kosher Culinary CLub. She teaches at a local Girls High School and gives several classes to ladies including the weekly Torah & Tea on Tuesdays.
