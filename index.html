Inspiring. Transforming. Guiding.

Learning is the foundation of Judaism, the food of the soul, and the fuel for spiritual growth in everyday living.

Regardless of background or affiliation, there is something here for you.

Our vision: Lifelong Jewish Learning

To create an intellectually stimulating space through which to share the depth, beauty and timeless wisdom of the Torah, in person or via a virtual classroom.

Making Divine Wisdom personally relevant and meaningful, this Academy will serve as a platform to broaden the knowledge and spiritual depths of any student, at any time, living anywhere on our planet.
